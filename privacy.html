Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Short version: BubblePop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything runs in your browser. No accounts, no cookies, no tracking.

What we collect

Nothing. BubblePop is a 100% client-side application. Your text, your bubble choices, and your exported PNGs never leave your device.

Local storage on your device

BubblePop uses your browser's localStorage to remember a few preferences between visits. This data stays on your device and is never sent to any server.

  • Theme preference (dark or light)
  • Language preference (French or English)
  • Your last 9 generated bubbles (for the history strip)

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser storage for getbubblepop.com, or by using the "Clear" button on the history strip.

Analytics

BubblePop uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to count anonymous page views. This service does not use cookies, does not fingerprint visitors, and does not store IP addresses. It is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, PECR, COPPA and LGPD without requiring a consent banner.

More info: cloudflare.com/web-analytics

Cookies

BubblePop does not set any cookies, first-party or third-party.

Third parties

At the time of writing, no advertising network, tracker, or third-party script is active on this site, beyond the privacy-friendly Cloudflare Web Analytics beacon. If we ever add discreet contextual ads, this page will be updated and the partners will be listed here.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because BubblePop does not collect any personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, export, or delete on our side. You have full and direct control over your local data through your browser settings.

For any question, contact us at inkup.app.contact@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the new version will be published on this page with an updated date above. Substantial changes will be announced in the app.
